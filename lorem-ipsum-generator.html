What is Lorem Ipsum and Why Do Designers Use It?

Lorem Ipsum is dummy text that has been the industry standard for placeholder content since the 1500s. It's derived from a Latin text by Cicero, but scrambled to create nonsensical content that looks like real text.

Designers use Lorem Ipsum because:

  • Focus on Design: It prevents clients from getting distracted by content
  • Realistic Text Blocks: It has normal word and sentence length distribution
  • Professional Appearance: It looks more professional than "text here" placeholders
  • Layout Testing: It helps test how designs handle real content length
  • Industry Standard: It's universally recognized in the design community

The History and Evolution of Lorem Ipsum

Ancient Origins

Lorem Ipsum comes from sections 1.10.32 and 1.10.33 of "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (The Extremes of Good and Evil) by Cicero, written in 45 BC. The text was scrambled to create Lorem Ipsum in the 1500s.

Modern Usage

Lorem Ipsum gained popularity in the 1960s with the release of Letraset sheets containing Lorem Ipsum passages. It became the industry standard with the advent of desktop publishing in the 1980s.

When to Avoid Lorem Ipsum

Consider using real content in these cases:

  • User testing and usability studies
  • Projects where content informs design decisions
  • Accessibility testing and compliance checks
