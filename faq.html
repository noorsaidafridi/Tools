General Questions

What is CalcTools Pro? +

CalcTools Pro is a collection of free online calculator tools designed to help with various calculations including BMI, percentages, loans, and tips. Our tools are easy to use, mobile-friendly, and completely free.

Are the calculators really free to use? +

Yes, all our calculator tools are completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or registration requirements. We support the site through advertising, but the calculators themselves remain free for everyone.

Do I need to create an account to use the calculators? +

No, you don't need to create an account or provide any personal information to use our calculators. They work immediately upon visiting our website.

Can I use CalcTools Pro on my mobile device? +

Absolutely! Our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The calculators are optimized for touch screens and mobile browsing.

Usage & Features

How accurate are the calculator results? +

Our calculators use standard, well-established formulas to provide accurate results. However, please note that:

  • Results are estimates based on the inputs provided
  • They should not replace professional financial or medical advice
  • Real-world factors may affect actual outcomes

We always recommend consulting with qualified professionals for important decisions.

Can I save my calculations? +

Currently, our calculators don't have a built-in save function since all calculations happen locally in your browser. However, you can:

  • Bookmark the page with your inputs (values remain in form fields)
  • Take a screenshot of your results
  • Copy and paste the results into a document

We're considering adding save features in future updates.

Do you plan to add more calculator tools? +

Yes, we're continuously working to expand our collection of calculator tools. We welcome suggestions from our users - if there's a specific calculator you'd like to see, please contact us with your idea!

Can I use these calculators for business purposes? +

Yes, you can use our calculators for both personal and business purposes. However, please review our Terms of Service for specific usage guidelines. Remember that results are estimates and should be verified for critical business decisions.

Privacy & Security

Is my data safe when using your calculators? +

Yes, your data is completely safe. Here's why:

  • All calculations happen locally in your browser - no data is sent to our servers
  • We don't store any of the numbers or information you enter
  • We use secure HTTPS encryption for all page loads
  • We don't require registration or personal information

Your privacy is our priority. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

Do you use cookies on your website? +

We use a minimal number of cookies for essential functions and analytics. These help us:

  • Remember your preferences (like dark mode if implemented)
  • Understand how visitors use our site to improve it
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

Calculator functions themselves don't use cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences through our Cookie Policy page.

Do you sell my data to third parties? +

No, we do not and will never sell your personal data to third parties. Since we don't collect personal information through our calculators, there's nothing to sell. We're committed to protecting your privacy.

Technical Issues

The calculator isn't working properly. What should I do? +

If you're experiencing issues with a calculator, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Refresh the page and try again
  2. Check that you've entered valid numbers in all required fields
  3. Try using a different web browser
  4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  5. Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser

If the problem persists, please contact us with details about the issue.

Why do I see advertisements on the site? +

CalcTools Pro is free to use, and advertising helps us cover the costs of hosting, development, and maintenance. The ads allow us to keep the calculators free for everyone without requiring subscriptions or fees.

We strive to keep advertisements unobtrusive and relevant. If you have concerns about specific ads, please let us know.

Can I embed your calculators on my website? +

Currently, we don't offer an embedding feature. Our calculators are designed to be used directly on our website. If you're interested in partnership or licensing options, please contact us to discuss possibilities.

Do you have an API for developers? +

We don't currently offer a public API for our calculators. However, we're exploring this possibility for future development. If you're a developer interested in collaboration, please reach out to us.
