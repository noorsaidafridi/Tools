Frequently Asked Questions

Are all tools on Mega Tool Kit completely free?

Yes, all tools on Mega Tool Kit are completely free to use. We don't charge any fees for using our tools. We support the site through advertising revenue.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No, you don't need to create an account or register to use any of our tools. They're available for immediate use without any sign-up process.

How accurate are the tools on your website?

While we strive for accuracy, our tools are provided "as is" without warranties. For critical applications, always verify results with reliable sources or professionals.

Can I suggest a new tool to be added to the website?

Absolutely! We welcome tool suggestions. Please use the contact form above to suggest new tools. We consider all suggestions and add popular requested tools when possible.

Do you store the data I input into the tools?

We do not permanently store the content you input into our tools. The processing happens in your browser, and data is not sent to our servers for most tools. For details, please see our Privacy Policy.

Why do I see ads on the website?

Ads help us keep the website running and all tools free for everyone. We try to ensure ads are non-intrusive and don't interfere with your tool usage experience.
