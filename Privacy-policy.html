Privacy Policy — CalcToolPro

Last updated: September 27, 2025

Home · Terms of Use

Welcome to CalcToolPro (the “Site”, “we”, “us”, or “our”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and tools at https://noorsaidafridi.github.io/Tools/.

Short summary: CalcToolPro does not collect personal data. Most tools run entirely in your browser and keep inputs local to your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We respect your privacy. Most CalcToolPro tools are built with standard web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and run in your browser. As a result:

  • We do not collect, store, or sell personal information about you.
  • User input entered into tools (text, numbers, files) is processed locally in your browser and does not leave your device unless you explicitly share it.
  • We do not maintain user accounts or databases for personal data.

2. Information That May Be Collected Automatically

Certain technical and non-personal information may be logged automatically by GitHub Pages or by analytics tools if you enable them, for example:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Visited pages and time on site

This information is typically anonymous and used to improve site performance and usability.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

Some tools may use localStorage or browser cookies to remember preferences, settings, or the last-used inputs for your convenience. This data is stored only in your browser and can be cleared by clearing your site data in the browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

CalcToolPro may use third-party libraries or CDNs (for example, for fonts or JavaScript helpers). These third parties may process minimal technical data (e.g., IP address) required to deliver content.

We do not control the privacy practices of third parties; please review their privacy policies if you have concerns about how they handle data.

5. External Links

The site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. When you follow external links, please review the privacy policies of those sites separately.

6. Children’s Privacy

CalcToolPro is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us to request deletion.

7. Data Security

Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages and benefits from GitHub’s hosting infrastructure. While we take reasonable steps to protect data, no online service is perfectly secure. Because most processing happens in your browser, we recommend that you do not upload or process extremely sensitive personal data with these tools.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will appear on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use of the site after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the site owner via the Contact or About section on the site, or visit the GitHub repository: https://github.com/noorsaidafridi.
